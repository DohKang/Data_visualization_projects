Revenue¶

  • Companys' yearly revenues in 2019, 2020, 2021, and TTM are plot and bar charted.
  • TTM refers to Trailing 12 Months, which represents the data of the past 12 consecutive calender months.
  • Although sales of the automobile company often refers unit sales, however, I used dollar amount here for comparison.

  • Tesla's revenue steadily increased every year
  • Toyota's revenue decreased from 2019 to 2021. However, TTM data represents that Toyota gains twice the amount of its revenue loss from 2020 to 2021.
  • GM graph varys slightly, but fairly consistent.

Market capitalization & outstanding share numbers¶

  • Market capitalization represents a total value of all of company's shares.
  • Outstanding shares represent that how many shares are issued at outstanding, it excludes treasury shares.
  • Market capitalization = price per share * outstanding shares
  • Stock price as of Jan 31th, 2023

    • TM = 146.67
    • GM = 39.32
